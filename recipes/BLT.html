Classic BLT Sandwich

The simple glory of a well done BLT

The BLT sandwich is a humble classic that too often gets overlooked.
Using fresh quality ingredients is key. Season the tomatoes well, use quality bread,
and don't skimp on the mayonnaise. If you are feeling really ambitious, try making your own mayo from scratch!.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices hearty, quality bread (sourdough, italian, wheat if you prefer)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Iceberg or Bibb lettuce, finely shredded
  • 3-4 slices thick cut bacon
  • 2-4 slices Beefsteak or heirloom tomato
  • salt and pepper
